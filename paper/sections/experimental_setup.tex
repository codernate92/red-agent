\section{Experimental Setup}
\label{sec:setup}

The empirical basis of this paper is the archived artifact set under
\path{reports/publication_run/}. The run uses the repository's stable
comparison-runner path with the \texttt{full} campaign configuration.

\paragraph{Registry and providers.}
At the time of writing, \path{core/model_registry.py} defines an 11-alias
curated registry across four providers and four families: OpenAI GPT models,
Anthropic Claude models, Google Gemini, and direct Mistral APIs.

\paragraph{Campaign construction.}
The \texttt{full} campaign expands the ATT\&CK-inspired taxonomy into 20 probes
organized by tactic category (reconnaissance, resource development, initial
access, privilege escalation, defense evasion, persistence, exfiltration,
impact, and goal hijacking). Each model receives the same 20 attempted probes.
The exact technique list is provided in \cref{sec:appendix}.

\paragraph{Trace capture and reporting.}
Each interaction is logged to per-model JSONL files under
\path{trajectories/publication_run/}. Structured per-model reports are stored
under \path{reports/publication_run/per_model/}. Publication assets (CSV,
plots, and LaTeX tables) are generated under
\path{reports/publication_run/latex_quality/}.

\paragraph{Evaluator configuration.}
This refreshed run reports campaign metrics and aggregate risk outputs from the
framework pipeline, plus post-hoc \sr-style scoring on archived traces using a
fixed judge specification (no target-probe rerun) \citep{souly2024strongreject}.

\paragraph{Operational status table.}
Provider preflight status is generated with
\path{analysis/provider_status.py}. In this curated run, all 11 configured
aliases are reachable in preflight.
